WELCOME TO TITAN'S CIRCUIT

RULES :-

THERE ARE TWO COLOURS RED AND BLUE, EACH ASSIGNED TO EITHER OF THE PLAYER

PLAYER CAN MOVE IT'S PIECES AFTER PLACING IT'S INITIAL 4 PIECES

MOVEMENT OF PIECES WILL ONLY BE STARTED IF ALL 4 PIECES OF EACH PLAYER IS PLACES

EVERY PLAYER GETS TOTAL 5 MINUTE TIME TO PLAY THE GAME

EVERY PLAYER GETS 20 SECONDS TIME TO PLAY THE MOVE

IF THE PLAYER DOESN'T PLAY THE MOVE IN 20 SECONDS THEN OTHER PLAYER WILL PLAY IT'S MOVE SKIPPING THE FIRST PLAYER'S MOVE

IF AN OPPONENT CAPTURES AN EDGE THEN POINTS ASSIGNED TO THAT EDGE WILL BE ADDED TO PLAYER SCORE

IF THE PLAYER LEAVES AN CAPTURED EDGE THEN THE POINTS OF THAT EDGE WILL REDUCED FROM THE PLAYER'S SCORE

PLAYER CAN MOVE IT'S PIECES TO INNER BY CONNECTED CIRCLES ONLY IF IT IS NOT OCCUPIED

PLAYER PIECE WILL BE ELIMINATED IF IT IS COMPLETELY SURROUNDED BY OTHER PLAYERS PIECES

IF A PLAYER GO OUT OF TIME, THEN THE OTHER PLAYER WINS

IF A PLAYER DOESN'T HAVE ANY VALID MOVES TO MOVE THE PIECE, THEN OTHER PLAYER WILL MOVE

IF THE INNERMOST HEXAGON GETS COMPLETELY FILLED, THEN THE PLAYER WITH HIGHEST SCORE WILL MOVE
